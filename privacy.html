Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how 2048: ColorWars ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and high scores
  • App preferences (theme, sound, game mode)
  • Purchase status for unlockable content

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob – displays advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. We request non-personalized ads only. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat – manages in-app purchases. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.
